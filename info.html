Are you tired of subscriptions?

I sure am. Creative workers are tired, and our subscriptions have expired. Your access to the tools you depend upon for sustenance and fulfillment erodes. You end up paying more over time. A-S-C is a public resource made by the creative community for the creative community. We advocate for equitable public access to digital tools through open-source, co-operative, and collective ownership models using software that is free, open-source, and a one-time fee—instead of subscription-based.

A-S-C seeks to grow regionally and globally in third spaces, schools, libraries, archives, tables, booths, parks, and websites — in the form of workshops or ways of self-learning together. We promote a creative diversity of tools, software, websites, and sovereign ad-hoc strategies.

We ask that you share A-S-C widely, and consider using this public resource in your creative process, collaborative workshops, and libraries (online and off). Perhaps you could promote a diversity of tools, software, websites, and approaches. Like, share, and unsubscribe! Say no to creative rent! Just A-S-C instead.
